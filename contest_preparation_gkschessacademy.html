Curriculum

A progressive curriculum designed for measurable improvement across openings, middlegame tactics and endgames. Emphasis on tournament formats and time controls.

  1. Assessment & Goal Setting

    Initial rating & strengths/weaknesses analysis to create a tailored plan.

  2. Opening Repertoire

    Build a reliable and practical opening set that suits the student’s style.

  3. Tactics & Calculation

    Daily tactical drills and visualization exercises to sharpen calculation speed.

  4. Endgames & Practical Play

    Essential endgame knowledge and practical plans to convert advantages.

  5. Mock Tournaments & Review

    Weekly mock events with detailed post-mortem analysis.
